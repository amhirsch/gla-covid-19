COVID-19 in Los Angeles County¶

Data courtesy Los Angeles County Department of Public Health. The archived daily press releases can be found here

Cases by Region¶

Collecting data at the county level is advantageous as the local health department releases statistics on COVID-19 cases by area. Such areas are broken down by cities, subsets of the City of Los Angeles, and unincorporated areas. The geographic boundaries are published at the County of Los Angeles GIS Portal. The regions are defined by the Los Angeles Times' Mapping L.A. project.

import math

import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import pandas as pd
import seaborn as sns

import lac_covid19.const as const

sns.set()

def adjust_x_ax(observations):
    min_date = observations[const.DATE].min()
    max_date = observations[const.DATE].max()
    plt.xlim((min_date, max_date))
    plt.xticks(pd.date_range(min_date, max_date, freq='SMS'), rotation=45)

def adjust_y_ax(observations, dep_var):
    TOP_MARGIN = 1.1
    NUM_TICKS = 5
    max_value = math.ceil(observations[dep_var].max() * TOP_MARGIN)
    step_size = math.floor(max_value / NUM_TICKS)
    plt.ylim(0, max_value)
    plt.yticks(tuple(range(0, max_value, step_size)))
